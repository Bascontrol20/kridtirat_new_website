Course Curriculum

Grasp the core concepts, business value, and project lifecycle of a PdM program. Learn about failure patterns, RUL, and calculating ROI.

Learn to process, clean, and transform raw sensor data. Covers time-series exploration, handling noise, and advanced feature engineering.

Build models that automatically identify when equipment is behaving abnormally using techniques like Isolation Forests and Autoencoders.

Master supervised learning to predict when a failure will occur using classification (XGBoost) and regression models.

Leverage advanced neural networks like LSTMs for superior performance on complex time-series sensor data.

Take your model from a research environment to a live production system. Learn about deployment strategies and model monitoring.

Hands-On Capstone Project

Apply everything you've learned in a comprehensive capstone project. You will receive a real-world dataset from a critical industrial component and build an end-to-end RUL prediction model, justifying your findings in a business report.
